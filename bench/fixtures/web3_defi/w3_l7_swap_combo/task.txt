Execute a 3-transaction DeFi combo on Sepolia: wrap ETH → approve WETH
→ swap WETH to USDC. All three transactions must be signed in MetaMask
one after another.

Steps:
  1. Click "Connect Wallet". Approve in MetaMask. Switch to Sepolia
     if prompted. MM password if locked: Testpassword1!
  2. Click "Wrap 0.005 ETH". Confirm in MetaMask. Wait for mining.
  3. Click "Approve 0.005 WETH". Confirm in MetaMask. Wait for mining.
  4. Click "Swap 0.005 WETH → USDC". Confirm in MetaMask. Wait for
     mining.
  5. Verify the outcome section turns green: "SUCCESS — combo complete.
     swap tx 0x..., USDC balance now N.NNN".

Finish with done PASS quoting the outcome. The swap tx hash (0x...) is
the critical evidence.

If any tx reverts or MM is rejected, done FAIL with a clear reason.
