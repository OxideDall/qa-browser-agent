Swap 0.01 WETH → USDC on Sepolia via Uniswap V3. Two transactions total.

Steps:
  1. Click "Connect Wallet". In MetaMask: approve the connection and, if
     prompted, approve the network switch to Sepolia.
     Password if MM is locked: Testpassword1!
  2. After connecting, the dApp shows your WETH balance (must be ≥ 0.01)
     and current router allowance (probably 0).
  3. Click "Approve 0.01 WETH". MetaMask opens a tx-approval popup —
     confirm it. Wait until approve tx status = "confirmed".
  4. Click "Swap 0.01 WETH → USDC". MetaMask opens another popup — confirm.
     Wait until swap tx status = "confirmed".
  5. The "Outcome" section will go green: "SUCCESS — swap tx 0x...,
     USDC balance now N.NNN".

Finish with done PASS quoting the outcome. The swap tx hash (0x...) is
the critical evidence. If either transaction reverts, use done FAIL with
a clear reason.
