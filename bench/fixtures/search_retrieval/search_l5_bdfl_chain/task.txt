          `https://mail.python.org/pipermail/python-dev/<YYYY-Month>/<NNNNNN>.html`.
          Extract both the year+month token (e.g. `2018-July`) AND the
          6-digit post number (e.g. `154547`).

  1. The PEP number (e.g. `PEP 572`).
  2. The year+month token from the resolution URL (e.g. `2018-July`).
  3. The 6-digit post number from the resolution URL (e.g. `154547`).

Example format:
  `done PASS "PEP 572 — Resolution: python-dev 2018-July, post 154601"`.

All three pieces (PEP number, year+month token, 6-digit number) must
appear verbatim in your description. If you cannot find the Resolution
URL, done FAIL with a clear reason.
