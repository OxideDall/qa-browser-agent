Chat with the EchoBot. It echoes every message back to you.

Send these THREE messages (in order), and verify each gets echoed:

  1. "hello"
  2. "how are you"
  3. "goodbye"

You can press Enter after each message or click the Send button. After
all three are echoed, the page data-attribute `data-msg-count` on
`#history` should equal 3, and `data-last-echo` should equal "goodbye".

Finish with done PASS quoting the third echo text verbatim — e.g.
`done PASS 'BOT echoed: "goodbye"'`. The word "goodbye" IS your evidence.

If the bot stops responding or messages fail to send, done FAIL.
