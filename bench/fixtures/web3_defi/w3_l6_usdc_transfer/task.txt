Transfer 1 USDC to a dead address via ERC-20 transfer on Sepolia.

Steps:
  1. Click "Connect Wallet". Approve in MetaMask (password if locked:
     Testpassword1!). Approve network switch to Sepolia if prompted.
  2. After connecting, the dApp shows your USDC balance. It must be
     at least 1 USDC.
  3. Click "Transfer 1 USDC". MetaMask opens a tx popup — confirm.
  4. Wait for the tx to confirm. tx status goes
     "sign-pending" → "mining" → "confirmed".
  5. Outcome box turns green: "SUCCESS — transfer tx 0x..., USDC
     balance now N.NNN".

Finish with done PASS quoting the outcome. The tx hash (0x...) is the
key evidence.

If the tx reverts or MetaMask is rejected, done FAIL with a clear reason.
