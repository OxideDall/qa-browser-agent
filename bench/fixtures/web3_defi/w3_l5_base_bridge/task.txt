Bridge 0.001 ETH from Sepolia to Base Sepolia via the official Base
L1StandardBridge contract.

Steps:
  1. Click "Connect Wallet". In MetaMask: approve the connection, and if
     prompted, approve the network switch to Sepolia.
     Password if MM is locked: Testpassword1!
  2. After connecting, the dApp shows your Sepolia ETH balance. It must
     be at least ~0.006 ETH (0.001 for the bridge + gas).
  3. Click "Bridge 0.001 ETH". MetaMask opens a tx-approval popup —
     confirm it.
  4. Wait for the L1 tx to confirm. tx status will go
     "sign-pending" → "mining" → "confirmed".
  5. The "Outcome" section turns green: "SUCCESS — L1 bridge tx 0x...
     confirmed; deposited 0.001 ETH to Base Sepolia".

Finish with done PASS quoting the outcome. The L1 tx hash (0x...) is
the critical evidence. The L2 credit to Base Sepolia happens
asynchronously and is verified by the fixture's on-chain assert — you
don't have to wait for it inside the dApp.

If the tx reverts or MetaMask is rejected, done FAIL with a clear reason.
