Sauce Labs demo store. The login screen shows valid credentials right
on the page; use:

  username: standard_user
  password: secret_sauce

After logging in you'll land on the inventory page. Add the FIRST
product (the Sauce Labs Backpack — should be the first card) to the
cart by clicking its "Add to cart" button, then verify the cart icon
in the top-right shows the badge "1". Call done PASS quoting that
badge value.
