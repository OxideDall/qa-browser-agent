This is a Todo list app. Add three new todo items by typing each into the input box and pressing Enter:

  1. Buy groceries
  2. Read a chapter
  3. Walk the dog

Then mark the SECOND item ("Read a chapter") as complete by clicking its checkbox.

After that, the footer should show "2 items left" and "3 total". Finish with done PASS quoting the footer text in your description.
